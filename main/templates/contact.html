FAQs

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Expedited (1–2 days) is available at checkout. Orders over $35 ship free.

What is your return policy?

Returns are accepted within 30 days. Books must be unread and in original condition.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! Gift wrapping is $4.99 per item. You can add a personalised note at checkout.

Can I request a specific book?

Absolutely. Send us the title, author, and ISBN and we can source most titles within 3–7 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

Visa, Mastercard, Amex, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are SSL encrypted.
